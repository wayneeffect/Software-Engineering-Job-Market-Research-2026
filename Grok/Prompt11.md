**Here’s a list of notable talent/staffing agencies specializing in software engineering** (including full-stack, frontend/backend, mobile, DevOps, cloud-native, and related development roles). These firms handle contract, contract-to-hire, permanent, and staff augmentation placements. Software engineering is one of the most active areas in tech staffing, with strong overlap to broader IT and computer engineering recruiters.

### Major National/Well-Known Agencies
- **Insight Global** — Premier developer staffing agency with a massive candidate database. Known for rapid placements (often 24-48 hours) of software engineers, full-stack developers, and specialized roles. Strong in contract and contract-to-perm.
- **TEKsystems** — One of the largest IT staffing firms, with extensive software development placements (frontend, backend, full-stack, cloud). Handles high-volume enterprise needs effectively.
- **Robert Half Technology** — Reliable leader with AI-powered matching for software engineers and developers. Covers temporary, contract, and permanent roles with broad national reach.
- **Apex Systems** — Strong tech staffing presence for software engineering and development roles across many industries.

### Specialized/Boutique Agencies
- **Redfish Technology** — Long-established (since 1996) specialist in software development recruiting. Focuses on SaaS, cloud, full-stack, and related engineering talent with contingent and retained options.
- **KORE1** — Precision recruiting for software engineers and engineering roles, with strong vetting and national coverage. Frequently ranked highly for results.
- **redShift Recruiting** — Tech-focused firm (founded by IT veterans) that personally vets candidates for software development roles. Emphasizes strong technical and cultural fit.
- **Alpha Apex Group** — Delivers software engineers with fast turnaround, high success rates, and cost efficiencies for startups and enterprises.
- **Motion Recruitment** — Specializes in mid-to-senior software engineering roles, with strong presence in major tech markets.
- **VALiNTRY**, **Nexus IT Group**, **180 Engineering**, and **Pearl Talent** — Notable specialists or strong performers in software developer staffing.

### Other Notable Mentions
- **Toptal** — Curated network of top freelance/independent software engineers (highly selective ~3% acceptance).
- **Near** — Strong for nearshore/LatAm software engineering talent.
- Executive/leadership-focused: **Christian & Timbers** (for VP Engineering, Staff/Principal Engineers, etc.).
- Regional or niche players like **TekRecruiter**, **Sage Recruiting**, **DevsData** (NYC), and others depending on location.

**Tips for choosing**:
- For **high-volume or rapid contract staffing** — Insight Global, TEKsystems, or Robert Half often excel due to scale.
- For **specialized or senior software engineering roles** — Boutiques like Redfish, KORE1, redShift, or Alpha Apex provide deeper vetting and better matches.
- Evaluate based on technical assessments, retention rates, time-to-fill, and guarantees (e.g., replacement periods).
- Check reviews on Clutch, ClearlyRated, or Glassdoor, and confirm expertise in your stack (e.g., specific languages, cloud platforms, or domains like fintech/healthtech).
